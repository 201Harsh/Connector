# Connector 🔗

**Connector** is a hyper-local job platform designed to bridge the gap between job seekers and nearby employers — enabling fast, simple, and accessible hiring.

---

## 🚀 Overview

Connector focuses on solving real-world employment challenges in local communities by making job discovery and hiring **instant and frictionless**.

Unlike traditional job portals, Connector is built for:

- Local businesses
- Small-scale hiring
- Real-time job access

---

## ✨ Features

- 🔍 **Local Job Discovery** — Find jobs near your location
- ⚡ **Quick Apply** — Simple and fast application flow
- 🏢 **Employer Job Posting** — Post jobs in minutes
- 🧠 **Skill-Based Matching** — Connect the right people to the right jobs
- 📱 **Mobile-Friendly** — Accessible on any device

---

## 🎯 Target Users

### 👨‍💼 Job Seekers

- Students (part-time jobs)
- Unemployed youth
- Skilled & unskilled workers
- Seasonal workers

### 🏪 Employers

- Cafés & restaurants
- Hotels & homestays
- Shop owners
- Local businesses

---

## 🧩 Problem It Solves

- ❌ Hidden local job market
- ❌ Slow and inefficient hiring
- ❌ Skill underutilization
- ❌ Migration due to lack of opportunities

---

## 💡 Solution

Connector provides a **centralized, easy-to-use platform** where:

- Employers post jobs quickly
- Job seekers discover and apply instantly
- Hiring becomes fast and accessible

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Frontend:** React / Next.js
- **Backend:** Node.js / Express
- **Database:** MongoDB
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS

---

## ⚙️ How It Works

1. User signs up
2. Creates profile (skills, experience)
3. Employers post jobs
4. Job seekers apply
5. Employers connect & hire

---

## 🌍 Vision

To become the **go-to local hiring platform** for tier 2 & tier 3 cities — enabling economic growth, reducing unemployment, and empowering communities.
